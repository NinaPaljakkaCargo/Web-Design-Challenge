City Data - Wind Speed

This plot shows wind speed relative to latitude. From this plot it can be observed that wind speed majority lies between 0 and 10 mph. It can also be seen that there are higher wind speeds in general on the lower and higher ends of latitute, further from the equator. The plot suggests that there may be some correlation between wind speed and latitude.
