City Data - Temperature

This plot shows maximum city temperature relative to latitude. From this plot it can be observed that it resembles a standard curve, having an upside-down "U" shape. This suggests that there is a relationship between lower maxiumum temperature and latitudes which are very high or very low, and the highest maxiumum temperatures are found closest to latitude of 0 degrees, or the equator.
